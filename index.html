SOILSAATHI

SOILSAATHI product description: SoilSaathi is a revolutionary AI-integrated product that delivers reliable, effortless, and accurate soil analysis directly on the farm. This portable device, designed like a backpack, allows the entire miniature testing lab to be easily transported to remote and challenging locations where traditional soil testing labs are inaccessible. Our advanced technology provides detailed soil profiles, facilitating smart nutrient management and offering customized crop recommendations for enhanced agricultural productivity.

Key Features of SoilSaathi include: AI Integration: Utilizes artificial intelligence for precise and reliable soil analysis. Portability: Compact and lightweight, designed like a backpack for easy transportation to farms and remote areas. Comprehensive Soil Testing: Measures pH, electrical conductivity (EC), macronutrients, and all micronutrients. Instant Results: Provides immediate soil test results, enabling quick decision-making in 20 minutes. Custom Recommendations: Delivers tailored fertilizer and crop recommendations based on soil profiles of each farm. Rugged Design: Built to withstand harsh field conditions where traditional soil testing labs are inaccessible.

No initial Calibration: Good to go model with no requirement of repetitive calibration. Self Cleaning: The design is equipped with easy cleaning modules within 2min. Cloud Interface: The tested results of soil sample are saved and recorded over cloud through a WEB Interface.

User-Friendly Interface: Easy to operate with intuitive navigation controls, displays and printing units. Real-Time Data Analysis: Offers live data for effective nutrient management and optimal crop planning. Enhanced Productivity: Supports improved agricultural practices by providing detailed soil insights. Sustainability: Promotes sustainable farming through accurate soil health assessments and resource management.

SOILLIFE

SoiLIFE product description: SoilLife is an innovative chamber designed to be inserted into the soil to accurately measure organic carbon, microbial activity, and greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. Equipped with state-of-the-art sensors, it delivers real-time data analysis of live soil conditions, including temperature, moisture, pH, and electrical conductivity (EC). This precise measurement of carbon emissions enables farmers to effectively manage their carbon credit scores.

The IoT-based SoilLife system offers live monitoring of extensive land areas, facilitating data-driven decision-making for agricultural agencies and B2B businesses. By harnessing cutting-edge technology, SoilLife promotes sustainable farming practices, enhancing soil health and productivity. Its seamless integration with existing farm management systems makes SoilLife an indispensable tool for modern agriculture.

Key Features of SoiLIFE include: Advanced Measurement: Accurately measures organic carbon, microbial activity, and greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions. Real-Time Data Analysis: Provides live analysis of soil conditions, including temperature, moisture, pH, and electrical conductivity (EC). Precision Carbon Monitoring: Precisely tracks carbon emissions to help farmers manage their carbon credit scores effectively. IoT Integration: Enables live monitoring of large land areas, facilitating data-driven decision-making. Sustainable Practices: Supports sustainable farming practices, enhancing soil health and productivity. Seamless Integration: Easily integrates with existing farm management systems for comprehensive soil management. User-Friendly Design: Intuitive interface for straightforward operation and data interpretation. Durable Construction: Robust design for reliable performance in various field conditions. Data-Driven Insights: Provides actionable insights for agricultural agencies and B2B businesses. Enhanced Productivity: Contributes to improved soil management and agricultural productivity through advanced monitoring capabilities.

SOILSPARSH

SoilSparsh:SoilSparsh is an advanced, portable device engineered to precisely measure moisture, humidity, and temperature, providing comprehensive monitoring of the surrounding environment. Designed for seamless integration with IoT-based setups and devices, SoilSparsh empowers farmers with real-time data and insights. This enables informed, onsite decision-making for optimizing smart irrigation management systems, ensuring efficient water usage and enhancing agricultural productivity.

The key features of device: Comprehensive Environmental Monitoring: Measures moisture, humidity, and temperature for thorough environmental assessment. Portable Design: Compact and lightweight, making it easy to transport and use in various field conditions. Real-Time Data: Provides immediate analysis and feedback for on-the-spot decision-making. WiFi Connectivity: Enables seamless connection to wireless networks for convenient data transfer and remote monitoring. IoT Compatibility: Integrates seamlessly with IoT-based setups and devices for enhanced connectivity and automation. Smart Irrigation Management: Supports intelligent irrigation systems by delivering precise environmental data, optimizing water usage. User-Friendly Interface: Simple to operate with intuitive controls and displays for ease of use. Durable Construction: Built to withstand harsh outdoor environments and rigorous use. Versatile Application: Suitable for diverse agricultural scenarios, from small farms to large agricultural enterprises. Enhanced Productivity: Helps improve crop yields and resource management through precise environmental monitoring. Sustainable Practices: Promotes efficient water usage and sustainable farming techniques by providing accurate environmental insights.
